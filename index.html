How To Play

Texas Hold'em with agent-assisted decisions.

  1. You receive two private cards. Five community cards arrive across flop, turn, and river.
  2. Use any five-card combination from your two cards and the board to make the best hand.
  3. Three local agents estimate strength, risk, and recommended action while automated opponents play against you.
